Why we exist

Real estate shouldn't be this complicated.

Most real estate transactions involve a fragmented collection of agents, escrow companies, lenders, and coordinators — often working in silos, rarely communicating clearly. The result is confusion, delays, and a process that feels out of your control.

Front Door was built to change that. We bring together escrow, buying, selling, and coordination under one roof — with one team, one point of contact, and one coherent process. Based in El Segundo and serving the broader South Bay, we work alongside clients who want more than a transaction. They want a partner.

Our approach is direct and personal. We don't hand you off. We don't overcomplicate. We show up, we listen, and we move the process forward with care.
