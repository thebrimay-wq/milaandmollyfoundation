About

All things dogs.
All heart.

Mila & Molly Foundation exists to care for dogs in the ways that matter most. From immediate rescue and medical attention to long-term sanctuary and loving foster support, we are building a foundation centered around compassion, healing, and hope.

We believe every dog deserves safety, dignity, and the chance to live a healthy, loved life.

What we do

What we do for dogs

01

Rescue & Charity

We help dogs in urgent need by providing rescue support, funding, and compassionate care where it is needed most.

02

Sanctuary

We create a safe, loving place for dogs who need stability, recovery, or long-term support.

03

Medical Care

We support dogs with the medical attention they need to heal, recover, and move toward a better life.

04

Foster Family Support

We believe healing happens best in loving environments. We help connect dogs with foster care and the support needed along the way.

Why it matters

Every dog deserves a safe place to land.

Some dogs need urgent help. Some need time to heal. Some need medical care, stability, or simply a place where they are finally safe.

At Mila & Molly Foundation, we are creating a place rooted in compassion — where dogs can be protected, cared for, and given the love they deserve.
